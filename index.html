A Little About Me

I'll be real with you. I don't have all the answers. The more I learn, the more I realize how much I still don't know. But that's never stopped me from showing up and putting in the work. When there's a problem in front of me, I don't walk away from it. I sit with it until I figure it out.

That's actually why I connect with Luffy so much. He doesn't chase power or try to one up anyone. He just wants freedom. The freedom to live on his own terms, to chase what matters to him, and to help the people he cares about do the same. He doesn't put restrictions on his crew. He lets them dream their own dreams. And the thing is, he's willing to go through hell for those dreams. Not because he's fearless, but because he already knows the worst that can happen and he's made peace with it. That kind of clarity is something I admire deeply and try to carry into my own life.

My journey started at Morgan State where I got my B.S. in Computer Science. From there I ended up at NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center working on landslide research with Python and geospatial tools. Real problems don't come with clean instructions. You just figure it out as you go. Now I'm an Associate Software Engineer at Freedom Technology Solutions Group, LLC. And honestly, a boy who sought freedom ending up at a company called Freedom? You can't make that up.
